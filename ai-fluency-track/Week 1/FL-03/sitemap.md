# REVISION (v2) — after the Claude pressure-test, 14 July 2026

The pressure-test found a real structural flaw: **About sat right before Scope Builder and answered a recruiter's question ("is she smart?") instead of the ops manager's question ("will she disappear after delivery?")** — a wobble at the exact moment of momentum toward the action.

## Changes applied

1. **Section 4 replaced: About → "How I Work"** (the single highest-impact change)
   - ✅ Availability status
   - ✅ Post-launch reality, stated truthfully: *I still actively support the DG Cement system today — I don't hand over and disappear*
   - ✅ One line on how scope changes are handled
   - Academic rank + certifications shrink to a single line near Contact/footer — off the conversion path
2. **Testimonial kept but labeled honestly** — the quote is real but the client won't be publicly named: *"Site Manager, DG Cement project (name withheld on request)"*. An honestly-labeled quote beats an unattributed-looking one.
3. **Work section re-weighted to ops-relevant six:** Inventory Management System (POS/audit logging), Customer Trends ETL, Mobile Expense Tracker, Global Sales Dashboard, + 2 strongest AI/data builds. **Compilers (UrduPy, SQL2Pandas, LALR) move behind "view all"** — impressive to engineers, focus-pulling for an ops manager.

## The map, revised

```
1. HERO        — claim + available badge → CTA
2. PROOF       — DG Cement case study + honestly-attributed quote
                 + "still supporting it today" line
3. WORK        — ops-weighted six, compilers behind "view all"
4. HOW I WORK  — kills the "will she vanish?" fear right
                 before the ask (replaces About)
5. SCOPE BUILDER
6. CONTACT     — one line of credentials here, not a section
```
