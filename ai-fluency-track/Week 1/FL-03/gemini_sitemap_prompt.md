# Master Prompt — Generate the Hand-Drawn Sitemap Sketch (Gemini)

> Use in **Gemini** (gemini.google.com) with image generation.
> Ask for a **wide/landscape** image if it gives you the option. If the first result has garbled text (common with AI images), reply: *"Regenerate — keep the layout but make ALL text labels large, clearly legible, and spelled exactly as written in my prompt."*
> Save the result as `sitemap-sketch.png` and drop it into §4.1 of `index.html`.

---

## The prompt (copy everything below)

Create a realistic photo-style image of a hand-drawn portfolio sitemap sketch, drawn in black ink pen on a slightly off-white sketchbook page, photographed from directly above in warm natural light. The drawing must look authentically hand-made by a design student: slightly wobbly rectangle boxes, imperfect handwritten block lettering, small ink smudges, a visible spiral binding on the left edge of the page.

At the top of the page, a handwritten title: "PORTFOLIO SITEMAP — SARA ARIF", underlined twice by hand.

Under the title, a small handwritten note in a hand-drawn box: "CLAIM: production systems that replace paper • PERSON: ops manager, industrial SME • ACTION: email me a scoped inquiry".

Below that, SIX hand-drawn rectangular boxes arranged vertically down the center of the page, connected by hand-drawn downward arrows, in this exact order with this exact handwritten text:

1. First box: "1. HERO — the claim + AVAILABLE badge"
2. Second box (drawn with a double border to look emphasized): "2. PROOF — DG Cement case study, 50+ workers, LIVE" with a small hand-drawn star next to it
3. Third box: "3. WORK — ops-weighted 6 (compilers → view all)"
4. Fourth box: "4. HOW I WORK — support after launch" — next to this box, a crossed-out word "ABOUT" with a small handwritten arrow note saying "changed after pressure-test!"
5. Fifth box: "5. SCOPE BUILDER — what process to replace?"
6. Sixth box (highlighted with a hand-drawn circle around it): "6. CONTACT — the ONE action: email inquiry"

Down the right margin of the page, three small handwritten stage labels with curly braces grouping the boxes: "LANDING" next to box 1, "BELIEVING" next to boxes 2–3, "TRUST" next to box 4, and "ACTING" next to boxes 5–6.

At the bottom of the page, a hand-drawn section titled "CUT ✂" followed by a short handwritten list, each item struck through with a single line: "services grid", "certificates wall", "skills chips", "experience timeline".

The overall style: authentic student notebook sketch, black ballpoint or fineliner ink only, no digital elements, no perfect straight lines, natural paper texture and soft shadows from the photo angle. All text must be clearly legible and spelled exactly as given.

## Fallback: draw it yourself in 5 minutes (always accepted)

If Gemini's text comes out garbled after 2–3 tries, just draw the six boxes on paper following the exact same structure above and photograph it — the brief literally asks for "a photo of your sitemap sketch," so a real sketch is the safest evidence there is. The cross-out of "ABOUT" → "HOW I WORK" is the detail worth keeping either way: it visibly proves the pressure-test changed something.

> ⚠️ Honesty note: if your track thread asks whether the sketch is hand-made, say you generated it with Gemini as part of your AI toolkit practice — that's fully in the spirit of this AI-fluency track, and hiding it isn't.
