# FL-01: Step-by-Step Assignment Completion Guide

This guide walks you through setting up your accounts, configuring your Claude Project, generating your screenshot evidence, and exporting your final PDF deliverable.

---

## Step 1: Set Up Your Tool Accounts & Academy Enrollment
You need accounts on three platforms. If you already have these, you can skip to Step 2.

1. **Claude (Anthropic):**
   - Go to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and sign up for a free account.
2. **ChatGPT (OpenAI):**
   - Go to [chatgpt.com](https://chatgpt.com) and sign up for a free account.
3. **Anthropic Academy:**
   - Go to the [Anthropic Academy](https://academy.anthropic.com/) site.
   - Register for a free account.
   - Enroll in the course: **"AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations"**.
   - Complete **Module 1: Foundations** (takes approximately 15–30 minutes of reading/quizzes).

---

## Step 2: Configure Your Claude Project
Claude Projects allow you to set custom instructions and context for your chats.

1. Log in to [claude.ai](https://claude.ai).
2. On the home page, look at the left sidebar (or click the menu) and select **Projects** (available on free and paid tiers).
3. Click **Create Project** (or **New Project**) and name it: `AI Fluency`.
4. In the Project dashboard, look for **Project Instructions** or **Custom Instructions** (sometimes under project settings).
5. Open the instructions box and paste the following custom instructions:

```markdown
[PROFILE & ROLE]
You are interacting with Sara Arif, a Computer Science student (CGPA 3.85, ranked 2nd) and AI Developer. She specializes in building multi-agent AI systems (LangGraph), compiler toolchains (UrduPy, SQL2Pandas), data systems, and full-stack software architectures (e.g., the PHP/MySQL DG Cement Work Tracker).

[TONE & STYLE PREFERENCES]
- Be extremely concise, technical, and code-first.
- Provide clean, production-ready code with minimal explanatory text unless asked.
- Avoid conversational filler (e.g., "Sure, I can help with that!", "Here is the code:").
- Structure responses with clear headings, bullet points, and code comments explaining design decisions.

[DEVELOPMENT STANDARDS]
- Python: Enforce LangGraph StateGraph practices, FastAPI modular routes, and clean Streamlit UI styling.
- PHP/MySQL: Prioritize database security (always use parameterized queries to prevent SQL injection).
- Compilers: Ensure correct AST parsing, tokenization rules, and formal optimization passes.

[CURRENT GOALS]
- Completing the General AI Fluency certification track.
- Scaling multi-agent EDA systems (DataSage) and optimizing LLM chat interfaces (AURA).
- Deploying robust, offline-capable PWAs for field workforce management.
```

6. Click **Save** or **Apply Changes**.

## Step 3: Capture the Screenshot Evidence
To satisfy the evaluation criterion: *"Tool accounts and Academy enrollment evidenced"*, you should capture two separate screenshots:

1. **Claude Project Configuration:**
   - Ensure your browser is displaying the Claude Project settings page with the custom instructions you just pasted.
   - Capture a screenshot of this area. On Windows, press `Win + Shift + S`.
   - Save the image on your computer as `claude_project_screenshot.png`.
2. **Anthropic Academy & ChatGPT Status:**
   - Log in to your Anthropic Academy account and go to the dashboard showing your enrollment in *AI Fluency: Framework & Foundations* or the Module 1 progress checkmark. (Alternatively, take a screenshot of your active ChatGPT chat/login page alongside it).
   - Capture a screenshot of this progress screen.
   - Save the image on your computer as `academy_evidence.png`.

---

## Step 4: Generate Your Final PDF Deliverable
Using the interactive dashboard created in your workspace:

1. Navigate to your workspace folder: `c:\Users\saraa\OneDrive\Desktop\FlyRank\Weel 1 AI fluency\`.
2. Double-click **`index.html`** to open it in your web browser (Chrome, Edge, or Firefox).
3. Review the page:
   - Ensure the student name is set to **Sara Arif**.
   - Check off the items in the **Tool Setup & Academy Progress** list.
   - Review the tasks in the **Workflow Audit** table. (You can double-click cells if you want to make any final adjustments to the wording).
4. Scroll to Section 4 (**Program Verification & Tool Evidence**).
5. Drag and drop `claude_project_screenshot.png` into the left dashed box, and `academy_evidence.png` into the right dashed box. The dashboard will instantly display both screenshots side-by-side.
6. Click the **Toggle Light Theme** button at the top (this switches the page to a clean, high-contrast light mode optimized for printing).
7. Click the **Print / Save as PDF** button. 
8. In the browser print dialog:
   - Set **Destination** to **Save as PDF** (or **Microsoft Print to PDF**).
   - Under More Settings, check the box for **Background graphics** to ensure the table badges and cards print with their borders/colors.
   - Click **Save** and name your file: `FL-01_AI_Workflow_Audit_Sara_Arif.pdf`.
