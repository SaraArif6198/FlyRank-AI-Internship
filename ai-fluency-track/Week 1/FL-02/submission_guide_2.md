## 1. How to Fill the Submission Fields

### Field 1: Deliverable Links (Choose One option)
*   **Option A (GitHub Link):** If you commit and push your assignment folder to your public portfolio GitHub repository, paste the direct URL to your `deliverable.md` here (e.g., `https://github.com/yourusername/your-repo/blob/main/Week%201%20AI%20Fluency%20assignment%202/deliverable.md`).
*   **Option B (Skip Link):** You can leave this blank if you choose to upload the files directly in the **Files** section instead.
